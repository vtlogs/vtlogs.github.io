Do you build log homes?
April '06
Answer: No. We do not build log homes; we simply sell the log home package. But, we do have a large list of local log home builders and contractors that we can recommend if you don't have one.
Do you charge extra for personalized design?
March '06
Answer: No. Our custom floor plan and design services are free to our customers.
Why do you recommend the Northern White Cedar package?
March '06
Answer: The Northern White Cedar we use is ideally suited for log home construction. It is naturally resistant to rot, mildew, and insects. It is light in weight, does not need to be force-dried, and is very stable. The professional craftsmen and log home builders we work with prefer it for these reasons.
