Tongue & V-Groove (TVG) profile

Northern White Cedar Tongue & V-Groove (TVG) panelling is beautiful, natural, and easy to install and maintain. It is the perfect choice for finishing interior walls, ceilings, and other building projects.

Sizes Available:
• 1" x 4" (8' - 12' lengths)
• 1" x 5" (8' - 12' lengths)
• 1" x 6" (8' - 12' lengths)


Horizontal TVG construction assembly.

Shiplap Boards

Northern White Cedar Shiplap panelling can create the same kind of look as the Tongue & V-Groove, but offers easier assembly and the choice of reversing the boards for a flush finish without the "V".

Sizes Available:
• 1" x 4" (8' - 12' lengths)
• 1" x 5" (8' - 12' lengths)
• 1" x 6" (8' - 12' lengths)
